Paying NYU Libraries Fines

NYU Bobst Library Items

Payments for Bobst, Bobst Offsite, or Bobst Reserve materials can be made:

  • Over the phone by calling (212)-998-2530
  • At the Bobst Circulation Desk during regular service hours
  • By appointment made on our reservation system

For any questions, call us at (212)-998-2530 or email us at lib-circulation@nyu.edu
